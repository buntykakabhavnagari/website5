Dev's Kitchen started as a small food cart with one big dream — to serve affordable, delicious, and soul-satisfying food to everyone. What began with a simple Maggi bowl has now grown into a beloved street food destination.

We believe great food doesn't need a fancy restaurant. Every dish is prepared fresh, with love and the finest ingredients — from our signature Maggi to creamy pastas and healthy bowls.
